Home Page

My name is Ahmet. I'm a self-learning programmer and indie game developer living in Turkey.

I am occupied with developing and publishing my own games, which include some 8bit-16bit style platformers and RPGs.

I've been actively working on these projects since 2022, learning programming and improving my skills at game design, drawing, pixel art, graphic design and sound design.


I work with C languages, mainly C#, but I highly regard the power of C++, especially in game development and desktop applications, the fields I'm most interested in.

I've had some experience with Unity, but ultimately switched over to MonoGame framework, at least for my own projects.

I've also been learning and practicing other fields of programming. I have been experimenting with Windows Forms, ASP.NET and HTML/CSS/JavaScript.


I wrote these pages in VS Code using HTML by myself to share some articles, showcase some of my works, and promote 'Obscure Games Studio' which is a name for my one-man gaming 'studio'.


The site consists of three sections:

Codlivion, where I may may share articles and guides on programming topics.

github.com/Codlivion - youtube.com/@codlivion

Obscure Games Studio, where I will be sharing news on my main game projects.

youtube.com/obscuregamesstudio

And Art Workshop, where I would share my drawing and audio samples, as well as some articles on the arts that I'm practicing.
