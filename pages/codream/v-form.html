As the world faces the danger of Ray Walter and his robots, one 'Variable Form' is there to stop him!

Play as V Form, the shape-shifting robot, who can morph into 5 different forms:

Standard Form: Blast your enemies with Bullets, Fire, Ice and Thunder.

Speed Form: Drive your way through in high speed.

Golem Form: Slow but sturdy. Defy the spikes and crushing blocks.

Claw Form: Crawl along the walls and ceilings. Slash your enemies.

Wing Form: Fly and unleash your breath of air.
